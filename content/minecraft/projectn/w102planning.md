Located at central to east plains of the island. This is a Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia themed city with some iconic buildings in Malaysia. Also featuring Malaysian style roads (elevated highway, roundabout, complex one-way streets) and terrace houses.

#### City

Located at south plains of the island. This is a well planned North America themed urban city with modern concept like aesthetics, blocky urban, green, safe, walkable, cyclist friendly, public transport, low-high building landscape curves. Also, each street has different style. This is inspired by creation of a YouTuber, Alpine.
